# Stremo Marketing & Growth Plan

## 🎯 Target Audience
- **Tech Educators**: Developers sharing live coding sessions.
- **Creative Professionals**: Designers and artists showcasing their process.
- **Micro-Communities**: Niche groups requiring high-quality, low-latency interaction.

## 📣 Marketing Strategy

### 1. Content-Led Growth
- **"Live Coding" Series**: Partner with top developers on X (Twitter) and YouTube to host exclusive streams on Stremo.
- **AI Highlights**: Use Gemini to auto-generate "viral clips" from streams to share on TikTok/Reels.

### 2. Community Engagement
- **Stremo Creator Fund**: Incentivize early adopters with financial rewards based on engagement.
- **Open Source Transparency**: Build in public. Share architectural decisions and roadmap updates to build trust.

### 3. Referral Engine
- **"Invite a Creator"**: Give both the referrer and the new creator premium features (e.g., higher bitrate, custom emotes).
- **Embedded Player**: Allow creators to embed their Stremo stream on their own websites, driving traffic back to the platform.

### 4. Strategic Partnerships
- **GCP Case Study**: Partner with Google Cloud to showcase Stremo as a premier example of media streaming on GCP.
- **Hardware Bundles**: Collaborate with camera and microphone manufacturers for "Streamer Starter Kits".

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **MAU (Monthly Active Users)**: Target 100k in Year 1.
- **Retention Rate**: Aim for >40% Month 1 retention for creators.
- **Average Watch Time**: Target 45 minutes per session.
- **Latency**: Maintain sub-2 second glass-to-glass latency globally.
